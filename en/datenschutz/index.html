Privacy Policy

This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect personal data on this website. It applies to all visitors and users of our website.

2. What Data Do We Collect and Process?

When you visit our website, the following categories of personal data are collected and processed:

Automatically collected data (by the web server):

  • IP address of the accessing device
  • Date and time of access
  • Browser and operating system of the device
  • Requested URL / page path
  • Referrer URL (originating page)

Data collected through the application form:

  • First and last name
  • Discord ID
  • Age
  • Player data (e.g. player name, statistics)

3. Purpose of Data Processing

The collected data is processed for the following purposes:

  • Operation and security of the website (automatically collected data)
  • Evaluation and processing of applications for membership in our gaming community
  • Contacting applicants in connection with submitted applications
  • Management of community members

4. Legal Basis for Data Processing

Data processing is based on the following legal grounds pursuant to Regulation (EU) 2016/679 (GDPR):

  • Art. 6(1)(f) GDPR – Legitimate Interests: For automatically collected data to ensure the security and functionality of the website.
  • Art. 6(1)(a) GDPR – Consent: For the processing of data from the application form, which you voluntarily provide by submitting your application.

5. Storage Location and Data Transfer

All collected personal data is stored on our own server operated by us. This server is located in Germany. No personal data is transferred to third countries outside the European Union or the European Economic Area.

6. Storage Duration

We store personal data only as long as necessary for the respective purpose:

  • Automatically collected access log data is automatically deleted after 30 days, unless it is needed for the security of the website.
  • Application data is stored for the duration of the application process. After completion of the process, it is deleted within 90 days, unless you are accepted as a member – in which case the data is stored for the duration of your membership.

7. Disclosure to Third Parties

We do not generally disclose personal data to third parties unless there is a legal obligation to do so (e.g. statutory disclosure obligations) or you have given your explicit consent.

8. Your Rights as a Data Subject

Under the GDPR, you have the following rights with regard to us as the data controller:

  • Right of access (Art. 15 GDPR) – You can find out what data about you is stored.
  • Right to rectification (Art. 16 GDPR) – You can have incorrect data corrected.
  • Right to erasure (Art. 17 GDPR) – Under certain conditions, you can request the deletion of your data.
  • Right to restriction of processing (Art. 18 GDPR)
  • Right to data portability (Art. 20 GDPR)
  • Right to object (Art. 21 GDPR) – You can object to processing based on legitimate interests.

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at: team.apx.r6@gmail.com

9. Complaint to a Supervisory Authority

You have the right to lodge a complaint with a data protection supervisory authority. The competent authority for Lower Saxony is the State Commissioner for Data Protection of Lower Saxony (LfDI).

10. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We reserve the right to change or update this privacy policy at any time. Changes will be published on this page. The policy in its current version shall apply from the time of its publication.
